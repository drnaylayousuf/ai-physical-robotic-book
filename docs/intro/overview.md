# Overview of Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This book explores the exciting and rapidly evolving fields of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. We will delve into the theoretical foundations, practical applications, and future potential of intelligent robots that interact with the physical world.

## Key Topics Covered

- **ROS 2 Fundamentals**: Learn the basics of the Robot Operating System 2 for building robust robot applications.
- **Simulation Environments**: Understand how to use tools like Gazebo and Unity for robot simulation and testing.
- **NVIDIA Isaac Platform**: Explore NVIDIA's platform for accelerating AI robot development, including Isaac Sim and Isaac ROS.
- **Vision-Language-Action Robotics (VLA)**: Discover how large language models (LLMs) are enabling robots to understand natural language and perform complex tasks.
- **Humanoid Specifics**: Dive into the unique challenges and solutions in humanoid robotics, such as kinematics, bipedal locomotion, and manipulation.
- **Conversational Robotics**: Investigate how to build robots that can engage in natural conversations with humans.
- **Capstone Projects & Lab Setup**: Apply your knowledge to real-world projects and learn about setting up a robotics lab.

## Target Audience

This book is intended for:

- Robotics enthusiasts and students.
- Software engineers interested in AI and robotics.
- Researchers and professionals working in automation and intelligent systems.

## What You Will Learn

Upon completing this book, you will have a solid understanding of:

- The core principles of Physical AI.
- How to design, simulate, and control humanoid robots.
- Integrating advanced AI techniques, including LLMs, into robotic systems.
- Practical skills for developing and deploying robotic applications using modern tools.
